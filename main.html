Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller that delves into the world of dreams. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, is given a chance at redemption if he can successfully perform inception, the act of planting an idea into someone's subconscious. As he assembles his team and ventures deeper into the layers of dreams, the lines between reality and dreams blur, leading to a mind-bending climax.
